Schedule

Our talks and speakers

The conference begins on Wednesday with an optional workshop day. We will have two options to choose from, to be announced soon.

Thursday and Friday are the main conference days where you will be able to view a morning keynote followed by six talk slots throughout the day. During each talk slot you'll have the choice between two different tracks. The day will then close with a further keynote and on Thursday our mid-conference social.

The full schedule will be announced in the first half of April, shortly after our Call for Papers closes.
